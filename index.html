Your Privacy Matters

Welcome to Nepali Ukhan! We believe that your personal business should stay exactly that—yours. We are committed to transparency and protecting any information you might worry about when using mobile applications.

Information Collection

We take a Zero-Data approach. Our app is designed to function without needing to know who you are.

  • Personal Identifiers: No names, emails, or phone numbers are collected.
  • Device Metadata: No IP addresses or unique device IDs are stored on our servers.
  • Usage Analytics: We don't track how you navigate the app or your individual preferences.

Push Notifications

With your permission, we may send you Push Notifications to share new proverbs or app updates.

  • One-Way Communication: Notifications are used only to send data to you. We do not use this access to pull any personal data from your device.
  • Control: You can opt-in or opt-out of these notifications at any time through your device's system settings.

Data Sharing & Security

Because we don't collect your data, there is nothing for us to share with third parties or advertisers. Your interaction with the app remains private and local to your device.
